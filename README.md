*A practical framework for modernizing complex enterprise application landscapes.*

---

## The Problem

Organizations operating long-lived systems often face:

- Fragmented legacy applications
- Unclear system-of-record ownership
- High operational and change risk
- Heavy reliance on tribal knowledge
- Difficulty prioritizing modernization efforts

Without a structured strategy, modernization initiatives become reactive, slow, and risky.

---

## Approach

This roadmap was developed using a **systems-first methodology**:

### 1. Application Inventory
- Identified business-critical systems across the enterprise
- Classified systems by function, lifecycle stage, and risk

### 2. System-of-Record Mapping
- Documented where authoritative data resides
- Identified manual handoffs, duplications, and integrations

### 3. Business Process Visualization
- Mapped end-to-end business flows using swim lanes
- Highlighted dependencies, decision points, and failure risks

### 4. Risk Analysis
- Evaluated systems using impact vs probability
- Prioritized modernization efforts based on organizational risk

### 5. Future-State Strategy
- Evaluated buy vs build options
- Aligned future systems to enterprise platforms
- Limited custom development to high-value gaps only
